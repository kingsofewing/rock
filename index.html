About the band

The Kings

We’re the Kings of Ewing from Evanston, IL (from one block on Ewing Ave.!). Whether it’s a classic rock singalong or the latest pop hit, we bring it all to life with energy and heart.

With a setlist spanning six decades of pop, rock, and everything in between, we keep the dance floor moving and the crowd singing along. Plus a couple originals thrown in for good measure.
