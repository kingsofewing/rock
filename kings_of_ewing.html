About the band

The Kings

We’re Kings of Ewing — a proud “dad band” out of Evanston, IL with a deep love for great music across every era. Whether it’s a classic rock singalong or the latest pop hit, we bring it all to life with energy and heart.

With a setlist spanning six decades of pop, rock, and everything in between, we keep the dance floor moving and the crowd singing along. Plus a couple originals thrown in for good measure.
